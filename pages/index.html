Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Bluetooth Chat App ("App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers.
  • We do not collect usage analytics.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or files.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

All messages exchanged within the App are transmitted directly between devices via Bluetooth and are not stored on any server.

3. How Data Is Used

The App functions entirely through direct Bluetooth communication between nearby devices.

  • Messages are transmitted locally between connected devices.
  • No data is sent to any external server.
  • No data is sent to the developer.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

We have no ability to access, monitor, or retrieve any messages exchanged within the App.

4. Data Storage

  • Messages are stored only locally on your device.
  • If you uninstall the App, locally stored data may be deleted.
  • We do not maintain backups or remote storage.

5. Permissions

The App may request Bluetooth permissions solely to enable device-to-device communication. The App does not request unnecessary permissions.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage providers
  • External APIs

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we do not collect data, there is no personal information to share or store.

8. Security

Because the App does not transmit data to external servers or third parties, the risk of remote data exposure is minimized. However, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date above.
